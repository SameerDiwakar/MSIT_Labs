About Najafgarh

My hometown is Najafgarh in New Delhi. It is a mix of village and city life. There are farms, markets, temples and many old houses.

Famous Places

  • Sai Mandir
  • Najafgarh Jheel
  • Main Market (aka "Delhi Gate of Najafgarh")
  • Chhawla Road

Street Food

  • Gol Gappe
  • Bhalle Papdi
  • Dahi Bhalla
  • Gajar ka Halwa (winter)

My Memories

I've lived here ever since I was born and playing cricket as a child is a memory I cherish.
