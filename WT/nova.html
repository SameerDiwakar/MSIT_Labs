About Dwarka

Dwarka is a well-planned residential sub-city located in South West Delhi. It is known for its wide roads, green parks, modern apartments, and excellent infrastructure. Dwarka is one of the largest residential areas in Asia.

Famous Places

  • ISKCON Temple Dwarka
  • Vegas Mall
  • Dwarka Sector 10 Market
  • DDA Sports Complex

Education Institutions

  • Delhi Public School, Dwarka
  • NSIT (Netaji Subhas University of Technology)
  • Mount Carmel School
  • GGSIPU University

Connectivity

Dwarka is well connected via the Delhi Metro Blue Line. It is close to Indira Gandhi International Airport and has excellent road connectivity.
