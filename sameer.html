About MSIT

This AICTE approved Institute under GGSIP University is ideally located in the Institutional area of Janakpuri, New Delhi. Established in 2001, MSIT has a campus spread over eight acres of land with beautiful eco-friendly surroundings. Institute was earlier operating from four storey building of 6279 m2 of built up area. Later it was shifted to a more spacious seven storeyed building with built up area of 17837 m2 The institute has over a short span of time acquired and developed impressive infrastructure
